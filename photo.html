Remote Sensing of Water & Carbon Lab

Group Introduction

  • Our Remote Sensing of Water & Carbon Lab at Tongji University focuses on understanding the links between water and carbon cycles at both regional and global scales, particularly in the cold region. We use a variety of methods to tackle the above challenges, particularly through integrating remote sensing with numerical models. We also do fieldwork and surveys in both the Tibetan Plateau and Arctic, which help us better understand the challenges in those areas we face today.
